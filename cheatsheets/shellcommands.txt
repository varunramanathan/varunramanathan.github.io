Restart internet-
CODE: systemctl restart network-manager.service

Download all .pdf files from a webpage:
CODE: wget -r -l1 -A.pdf http://www.example.com/page-with-pdfs.htm

Switchoff touchscreen:
CODE: xinput set-prop 10 "Device Enabled" 0
This works if your touchscreen is device number 10. To check your touch-screen input device number:
CODE: xinput --list
This can be used for any input device from the list.
